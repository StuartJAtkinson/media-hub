## Integration Roadmap

> **Aim:** Pull in functionality from the projects listed below, take inspiration
> from open-source and commercial alternatives, analyse their features and integrate
> the best ideas into this software.
>
> **Long-term goal:** 2-way sync compatibility with the open-source and commercial
> alternatives listed here, so data and workflows move freely in both directions.

### My projects to absorb

| Project | What it contributes |
|---------|---------------------|
| [socialMediaArchiver](https://github.com/StuartJAtkinson/socialMediaArchiver) | Archive and preserve posts from social media platforms |
| [YouTubeCommunityPosts](https://github.com/StuartJAtkinson/YouTubeCommunityPosts) | Download and archive community posts from YouTube channels |
| [simklExporter](https://github.com/StuartJAtkinson/simklExporter) | Export watch history and ratings data from Simkl to portable formats |
